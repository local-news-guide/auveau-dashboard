What Morpheus8 Treats

Morpheus8 penetrates deeper into the skin than any other microneedling device — up to 4mm. It stimulates collagen production in the underlying dermis, reorganizing the building blocks of your skin through a natural anti-aging process.

  • Loose or sagging skin
  • Jowls and jawline

  • Fine lines

  • Stretch marks

Morpheus8 is safe and effective for all skin types and tones.

How Morpheus8 Works

Morpheus8 is a fractional skin treatment that delivers radiofrequency energy through an array of ultra-sharp micro-needles. The needles create tiny punctures in the skin while the RF energy heats the deeper tissue layers, triggering your body's natural collagen and elastin production.

Because the needles are sharper and the punctures are smaller than traditional microneedling, patient comfort is significantly improved — no tugging, no pulling. Your provider adjusts the depth and energy level for each treatment zone, so the session is tailored precisely to your anatomy and goals.

Treatment Areas

Face and Jawline

The most popular Morpheus8 target. Tightens jowls, defines the jawline, and smooths wrinkles across the lower face and cheeks. Visible lifting without surgery.

Neck and Under-Chin

Addresses the submental area — the space below the chin where skin tends to loosen first. Tightens and contours for a more defined profile.

Body

Tightens loose skin on the inner and outer thighs, above the knees, and across the abdomen. Reduces the appearance of cellulite and crepey texture.

Scars and Texture

Smooths acne scars, surgical scars, stretch marks, and enlarged pores. The deep RF energy remodels scar tissue from below the surface for lasting improvement.

Your Treatment, Start to Finish

Step 1 — Consultation

Your provider evaluates your skin concerns, discusses your goals, and designs a treatment plan. The needle depth and RF energy settings are customized for each area being treated.

Step 2 — Treatment

A numbing cream is applied to the treatment area before the session begins. The Morpheus8 handpiece delivers RF energy through micro-needles at your customized depth. Most sessions take 30 to 60 minutes depending on the areas treated.

Step 3 — Recovery and Results

Expect mild swelling and redness similar to a sunburn for 3 to 4 days. Results begin to appear after the first treatment and continue improving for up to three months as collagen rebuilds. Most patients achieve optimal results after 3 sessions spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart.

Common Questions

How does Morpheus8 work?
Morpheus8 combines radiofrequency energy with microneedling to stimulate collagen production in the deeper layers of the skin. The micro-needles deliver RF energy at adjustable depths up to 4mm, remodeling tissue from the inside out.
How quickly will I see results?
You will see initial improvement after your first treatment. Full results develop over the following weeks as collagen remodels, with optimal results typically visible after 3 sessions spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart. Results continue improving for up to three months after treatment.
What is the recovery like?
Expect mild swelling and redness similar to a sunburn for 3 to 4 days. Some flaking may occur as the tiny punctures heal. Most patients return to normal activities within a few days.
Is Morpheus8 safe for all skin types?
Yes. Unlike some laser treatments, Morpheus8 is safe and effective for all skin types and tones. This is one of the key advantages of the technology.
What areas can be treated?
Morpheus8 treats the face, jawline, neck, under-eyes, decolletage, arms, knees, abdomen, and thighs. It addresses loose skin, wrinkles, acne scars, stretch marks, cellulite, and discoloration.
How many treatments do I need?
Most patients achieve optimal results with 3 sessions spaced 4 to 6 weeks apart. Your provider at Auveau will recommend a plan based on your specific concerns, the areas being treated, and your available recovery time.
