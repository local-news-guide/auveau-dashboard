General
What is a medical spa, and how is Auveau different from a regular spa?

A medical spa (medspa) combines clinical-grade treatments with the comfort of a spa environment. At Auveau, every treatment plan is designed and overseen by licensed medical professionals, including our lead provider Chrissy Gray Lim, PA-C. Unlike a day spa, we offer injectables, prescription-strength lasers, and medically guided wellness treatments — all in a boutique setting that feels anything but clinical.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

We start every new patient relationship with a personalized consultation. During your visit, your provider will assess your skin, listen to your goals, and recommend a treatment plan tailored to your anatomy and lifestyle. We never use a one-size-fits-all approach. Book a consultation online or by calling (512) 840-0607.

How do I prepare for my first appointment?

Arrive with clean skin (no makeup on the treatment area if applicable). Avoid blood-thinning medications and supplements for 1 week before injectables (consult your doctor first). For laser treatments, avoid sun exposure and self-tanners for 2 weeks prior. Shave the treatment area the day before laser hair removal — do not wax or pluck.

Injectables
How much does Botox cost at Auveau?

Botox pricing at Auveau is based on the number of units used, which varies by treatment area and individual anatomy. We provide exact pricing during your consultation so there are no surprises. We also offer membership plans that include Botox at reduced rates.

What is the difference between Botox and dermal fillers?

Botox relaxes muscles that cause dynamic wrinkles — forehead lines, crow's feet, frown lines. Dermal fillers add volume where it has been lost: cheeks, lips, nasolabial folds, jawline. Many patients benefit from both treatments used together. Your provider will recommend the right combination based on your facial anatomy and goals.

How long do Botox results last?

Botox typically lasts 3-4 months. With consistent treatments, many patients find their results last longer over time as the treated muscles become conditioned. We recommend scheduling your next appointment before your results fully wear off to maintain smooth, natural-looking results.

Laser & Skin Treatments
How many laser hair removal sessions will I need?

Most patients achieve significant permanent hair reduction in 4-8 sessions, spaced 4-6 weeks apart. Results depend on hair color, skin type, and the treatment area. Our LightSheer Diode laser is the gold standard in hair removal technology and delivers faster, more comfortable treatments than older systems.

Is Morpheus8 painful, and what is the downtime?

With topical numbing cream applied before treatment, most patients describe Morpheus8 as a tolerable warm sensation. Expect 2-3 days of mild redness and swelling. Most people return to normal activities within 48 hours. Results improve progressively over 3-6 months as new collagen forms.

Which facial treatment should I start with?

For most patients, the HydraFacial is an excellent starting point — it cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates in one 30-minute session with no downtime. For more advanced concerns like acne scarring or hyperpigmentation, your provider may recommend chemical peels or microneedling.

Body & Wellness
Does Emsculpt actually work?

Yes. Clinical studies show an average 16% increase in muscle mass and 19% reduction in fat after a full Emsculpt treatment series (typically 4 sessions). Using HIFEM technology, Emsculpt triggers 20,000 muscle contractions in 30 minutes — not achievable in any gym session. Most patients see and feel results after 2-3 sessions.

What does hormone replacement therapy involve?

Auveau's BHRT program uses EvexiPEL bio-identical hormones derived from natural plant-based substances. After comprehensive lab work and consultation, your provider creates a customized hormone plan to address symptoms like fatigue, weight fluctuations, mood changes, and reduced libido. Treatment typically involves pellet insertion every 3-6 months.
