Your Treatment, Start to Finish

Step 1 — Consultation

We begin with a one-on-one conversation about your goals. Your provider evaluates your facial structure, muscle movement, and skin quality to recommend a personalized plan. No pressure, no upselling — just honest guidance.

Step 2 — Treatment

Botox and Dysport injections take about 15 to 20 minutes. Most patients describe the sensation as a quick, light pinch. No anesthesia is needed, and there is no downtime. You can return to your day immediately.

Step 3 — Results

You will begin to see smoothing within a few days. Full results develop over 7 to 14 days as the neurotoxin settles into the treated muscles. Results typically last 3 to 6 months, and maintenance appointments keep your results consistent over time.

Which Neurotoxin Is Right for You?

Botox

FDA-approved neurotoxin that offers precise, targeted wrinkle reduction. Ideal for smaller treatment zones like crow's feet, lip lines, and brow lift. Results appear within 7 to 14 days and typically last 3 to 6 months.

Dysport

FDA-approved neurotoxin that spreads slightly more than Botox, making it well-suited for larger treatment areas like the forehead. Some patients notice results as early as 2 to 3 days. Lasts 3 to 6 months.

Your provider will recommend the best option based on your anatomy, treatment goals, and how your body has responded to past treatments.

Common Questions

How long does a Botox or Dysport appointment take?
Most treatments take 15 to 20 minutes from start to finish. There is no required preparation or recovery time.
Does it hurt?
Most patients describe the sensation as a quick pinch, similar to a light rubber band snap. It is brief and very manageable. No anesthesia is needed.
When will I see results?
You will notice initial smoothing within a few days. Full results typically appear within 7 to 14 days after treatment.
How long do results last?
Botox and Dysport results generally last 3 to 6 months. Regular maintenance appointments help keep results smooth and consistent.
Will I still look like myself?
Yes. Our approach is intentionally conservative. We treat to soften, not to freeze. You will still make every natural expression — without the lines.
How is Botox priced?
Botox and Dysport are priced by the unit. The number of units you need depends on the areas treated and the strength of the muscles involved. Your provider will walk you through pricing during your consultation.
