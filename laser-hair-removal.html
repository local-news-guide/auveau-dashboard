Common Questions

Frequently asked

How many sessions do I need?
Most patients see significant permanent hair reduction in 4–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart. The exact number depends on hair color, skin type, and the treatment area. Consistency is key to permanent results.
Is laser hair removal painful?
Most patients tolerate the procedure well. The LightSheer’s patented Sapphire ChillTip continuously cools the skin during treatment. If you’re concerned about discomfort, arrive 20–30 minutes early to apply topical numbing cream.
How long does each session take?
Session time varies by area. Upper lip: under 1 minute. Underarms: about 5 minutes. Full legs or full back: up to 45 minutes. The LightSheer’s high-speed pulses make sessions significantly faster than older systems.
Can I shave between sessions?
Yes — shaving is encouraged. Do not wax, pluck, or use depilatory creams, as these remove the hair follicle that the laser needs to target. Shave the treatment area the day before your appointment.
Does it work on all skin tones?
The LightSheer Diode is effective across a wide range of skin tones. It works best on dark, coarse hair. During your consultation, your provider will assess your skin type and hair characteristics to confirm candidacy and set realistic expectations.
