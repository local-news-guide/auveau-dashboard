What Juvederm Treats

Juvederm is a collection of hyaluronic acid dermal fillers, each designed for specific areas of the face. Whether you are looking to smooth deep lines, restore cheek volume, or enhance your lips, there is a Juvederm formulation built for that purpose.

  • Nasolabial folds
  • Smile lines
  • Marionette lines
  • Lip enhancement
  • Lip lines
  • Cheek contouring
  • Under-eye hollows
  • Jawline definition
  • Facial volume loss

Your provider will recommend the right Juvederm product — or combination — based on the areas that matter most to you.

How Juvederm Works

As we age, our natural levels of hyaluronic acid decrease. This leads to reduced volume, deeper folds, and hollowing in areas that once looked full and defined. Juvederm delivers smooth, gel-based hyaluronic acid directly into the targeted area to restore that lost volume.

The result is immediate. You will see visible improvement right after treatment. Because hyaluronic acid occurs naturally in your skin, Juvederm integrates smoothly and feels natural to the touch.

Your Treatment, Start to Finish

Step 1 — Consultation

Your provider sits with you to understand what you would like to achieve. Together, you will identify which areas to treat and which Juvederm products will deliver the results you are looking for. No rush, no pressure.

Step 2 — Treatment

Juvederm is administered through a smooth-flowing injection that gives your provider precise control for natural facial contouring. Numbing cream or Lidocaine-infused formulas keep the experience comfortable. Most sessions take 15 to 30 minutes.

Step 3 — Results

Results are visible immediately. You will see restored volume, smoother contours, and a refreshed appearance right after your session. Depending on the product used, results last from 9 months to 2 years.

Common Questions

How does Juvederm work?
Juvederm delivers hyaluronic acid — a substance that occurs naturally in your skin — into targeted areas to restore volume, smooth wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. Results are visible immediately after treatment.
How long does Juvederm last?
Results vary by product. Juvederm Ultra lasts up to 9 months, Volbella up to 12 months, Vollure up to 18 months, and Voluma up to 2 years.
Is Juvederm painful?
Most patients find the procedure very manageable. Numbing cream may be applied before treatment, and Juvederm XC formulations contain Lidocaine for added comfort during injection.
What areas can Juvederm treat?
Juvederm treats nasolabial folds, smile lines, lip lines, marionette lines, thin lips, under-eye hollows, and cheek volume loss. Each product in the Juvederm collection is designed for specific areas.
Is Juvederm safe for all skin types?
Yes. Juvederm is the only approved hyaluronic acid dermal filler that has demonstrated its safety and effectiveness in patients of color.
How long does an appointment take?
Most Juvederm treatments are completed in 15 to 30 minutes depending on the areas being treated. Results are practically instantaneous.
